How to Play?

It's a breeze to become a Freezeria pro! Here's the scoop:

  1. Take Orders: Listen carefully to what your customers want.
  2. Build the Base: Scoop the right ice cream flavors into a cup.
  3. Blend It Up: Mix those flavors to perfection at the blender station.
  4. Top It Off: Add whipped cream, sauces, and sprinkles to create a masterpiece.
  5. Serve with a Smile: Deliver the order to your happy customer and earn tips!
